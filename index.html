Agenda

Fostering dialogue, action, and change.

  • Friday, 17 January 2020:

    Optional city tour at 4 pm

    Meet at Paradeplatz to explore Zurich with a free guided tour by Free Walk Switzerland.

  • Friday, 17 January 2020

    Welcome meal at 6:30 pm

    We have our welcome dinner at Restaurant Kantorei, Neumarkt 2, 8001 Zürich. The meal is included in the participation fee, alcoholic drinks are on you.

  • Saturday, 18 January 2020:

    Day conference at 9 am

    We discuss how to improve global collaboration in the areas climate emergency, technology governance and global health at the PwC Experience Center, Rieterstrasse 6, 8002 Zürich.

  • Saturday, 18 January 2020:

    Farewell party at 7 pm

    We have dinner with a catering by the Restaurant Gärtnerei and a subsequent party with live DJ at Werkstadt Zürich, Hohlstrasse 400, 8048 Zürich. All covered by the participation fee (including drinks).

  • Sunday:
    Departure
    to Davos

About

The spirit of Davos in Zurich.

Ahead of the World Economic Forum Annual Meeting in Davos from 21 to 24 January 2020, the next generation of global leaders is coming together in Zurich on 17 and 18 January 2020. The Pre-Davos Summit connects 90 Global Shapers from more than 40 countries to find solutions to the world's most pressing problems. The 50 Shapers selected by the World Economic Forum to participate in the Annual Meeting leverage the Pre-Davos to discuss global affairs with 40 peers from Switzerland and refine their plans for improving the state of the world in Davos.

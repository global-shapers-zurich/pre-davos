About

Learn what the Pre-Davos is all about.

Ahead of the 2020 World Economic Forum in Davos (21 – 24 January 2020), the next generation of global leaders is coming together in Zurich (17 – 19 January 2020). The Pre-Davos connects 90 participants from more than 35 countries, bringing together globally recognized visionaries selected to participate in the Annual Meeting of the World Economic Forum in Davos as well as Global Shapers from across Switzerland – a group of ambitious, locally engaged, and diverse young pioneers in their respective fields.

As part of their preparation for the WEF Annual Meeting, the 40 young visionaries selected to participate in Davos will leverage the Pre-Davos to exchange ideas with Global Shapers from Switzerland, discussing and refining their plans for improving the state of the world and for driving dialogue, action, and change in Davos.
